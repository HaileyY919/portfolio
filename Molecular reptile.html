Molecular Reptile Tabletop Poster

My dad owns his own business called Molecular Reptile where he breeds and sells snakes and other reptiles. For one of his reptile shows he wanted me to make him a poster he could put on his booth table. He wanted his logo to be in the center and have a few of his snake pictures featured in it. This poster is what I created. Later on, as my skills improved he asked me to make him a full sized banner. He wanted the banner to essentially be a larger version of the table-top poster.
